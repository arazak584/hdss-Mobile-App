Denominations


1. Protestant — comprises AME Zion, Anglican, Baptist Church, Evangelical
Presbyterian Church, Lutheran, Methodist Church Ghana, Presbyterian,
    Seventh Day Adventist (SDA), etc.


2. Pentecostal/Charismatic — includes Action Chapel, Assemblies of God, Christ
Apostolic Church, Foursquare Gospel Church, Global Evangelical Church,
Harvest Chapel Int., International Bible Worship Centre, International Central
Gospel Church (ICGC), Jubilee International Church, Lighthouse Chapel,
Perez Chapel, Praise Valley Temple, Rhema Christian Centre, Salvation Army,
    The Apostolic Church, The Church of Pentecost, Victory Bible Church, etc.


3. Other Christian — includes members of the African Faith Tabernacle Church,
Church of Christ, Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Church Universal
and Triumphant, Jehovah’s Witnesses, Kristo Asafo, Musama Disco Christo
    Church, Twelve Apostles Church, etc.
